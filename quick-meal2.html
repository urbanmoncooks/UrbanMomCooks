Quick Meal 2: Stir-Fry Delight

A quick and flavorful stir-fry recipe that's ready in no time. Ideal for a busy evening.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken or tofu
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce and seasoning

Instructions:

  1. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add oil.
  2. Add chicken or tofu and cook until browned.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  4. Season with soy sauce and your choice of seasoning.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
