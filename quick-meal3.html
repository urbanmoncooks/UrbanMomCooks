Quick Meal 3: Breakfast Smoothie

A nutritious and quick breakfast smoothie to kickstart your day with energy and taste.

Ingredients:

  • Yogurt or milk
  • Fruits (bananas, berries)
  • Honey or sweetener

Instructions:

  1. Combine yogurt or milk with your choice of fruits in a blender.
  2. Add honey or sweetener to taste.
  3. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  4. Pour into a glass and enjoy your nutritious breakfast smoothie!
