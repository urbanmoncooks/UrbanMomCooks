Recipe 2: Chicken and Veggies

A healthy and quick chicken and vegetable stir-fry. Packed with nutrition and flavor.

Ingredients:

  • 2 boneless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add chicken, and cook until browned.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for a few minutes.
  4. Add soy sauce and stir well.
  5. Serve your delicious Chicken and Veggies stir-fry!
