Recipe 1: Delicious Pasta

A quick and delicious pasta recipe that your kids will love. Perfect for a busy weeknight dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 250g pasta
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheese

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Heat the tomato sauce in a pan.
  3. Combine cooked pasta with the sauce and mix well.
  4. Sprinkle shredded cheese on top and cover until it melts.
  5. Serve and enjoy your delicious pasta!
