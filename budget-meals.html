Budget-Friendly Meals

Budget Meal 1: Spaghetti with Tomato Sauce

A simple and delicious budget-friendly meal that your family will love. Perfect for busy weeknights.

  • Ingredients: Pasta, tomato sauce, garlic, olive oil, herbs
  • Instructions: Boil pasta, sauté garlic, mix with tomato sauce, add herbs, and serve.

Budget Meal 2: Rice and Beans

An affordable and nutritious meal that's quick to prepare. Perfect for those on a tight budget.

  • Ingredients: Rice, black beans, onion, spices, salsa
  • Instructions: Cook rice, sauté onions, add beans, spices, and salsa, and enjoy.

Budget Meal 3: Vegetable Stir-Fry

A low-cost and healthy vegetable stir-fry that's easy to make. Great for those watching their budget.

  • Ingredients: Mixed vegetables, tofu or chicken, soy sauce, garlic
  • Instructions: Stir-fry veggies, add tofu/chicken, soy sauce, and garlic, and serve.
