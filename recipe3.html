Recipe 3: Fruit Salad

An easy and refreshing fruit salad that your kids will enjoy as a healthy dessert or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 1 apple, diced
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 cup mixed berries

Instructions:

  1. Wash and prepare the fruits.
  2. Dice the apple and slice the banana.
  3. Combine all the fruits in a bowl.
  4. Toss gently and serve your refreshing Fruit Salad!
