Quick Meal 1: Tasty Sandwich

A super-quick and delicious sandwich recipe perfect for a speedy lunch or dinner.

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 4-6 slices of turkey or ham
  • Lettuce leaves
  • 1 ripe tomato, thinly sliced
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Spread mayonnaise and mustard on one side of each bread slice.
  2. Layer the turkey or ham slices, lettuce, and tomato on one slice of bread.
  3. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, condiments side down.
  5. Cut the sandwich in half diagonally and serve.

Enjoy your quick and tasty sandwich!
